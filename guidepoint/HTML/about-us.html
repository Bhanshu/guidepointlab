Knowmore about GuidepointLabs

GuidePoint Labs is a comprehensive diagnostic testing center offering laboratory testing services Worldwide. We offer clinical testing for a wide array of medical conditions with a focus on non-invasive diagnostic testing (lab testing performed from saliva and urine). In addition, we provide Clinical DNA Testing, DNA Banking, Clinical Research and Telemedicine Services. Our highly experienced Medical and Laboratory Team aims to provide fast turnaround times, outstanding personalized service and the most affordable prices in medical laboratory services.
