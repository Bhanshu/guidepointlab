ASR Compliance Statement

Analyte specific reagents (ASR) are used in many laboratory tests necessary for standard medical care and generally do not require U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) approval or clearance. Quality Assurance

GUIDEPOINT LABS COMMITMENT TO QUALITY ASSURANCE

Quality is of utmost importance at GUIDEPOINT. It is a process and goal that permeates every department and technical section within GUIDEPOINT. Quality improvement programs are used at GUIDEPOINT to improve patient care and to address the needs of its clients. Through constant monitoring and with a focus on continual improvement, GUIDEPOINT ensures that clients are satisfied with the service they receive.

To achieve and maintain high standards, each test undergoes pre and post analytical testing and all tests have included positive and negative controls and in some cases are performed with redundancy. We often perform post-testing calls to our clients to evaluate the accuracy and clarity of laboratory reports; appropriateness of samples, labeling, and transport conditions; assay precision; client interaction and consultation; compliance with policy and procedure; patient outcome; and turnaround time.

GUIDEPOINT views quality assurance as the natural cornerstone to continuous improvement. By utilizing the data generated by QA indicators, combined with internal and external customer needs assessments, Continuous Quality Improvement (CQI) uses client and employee involvement to improve critical processes. The goal is to define and meet the needs of GUIDEPOINTâ€™s complex customer base. CQI allows GUIDEPOINT to constantly reevaluate and improve customer service.

GUIDEPOINT is committed to providing the highest quality service to its clients in full compliance with all federal, state, and local laws and regulations. This commitment applies not only to technical operations, but also to business operations, including marketing practices and billing. GUIDEPOINT seriously evaluates its ethical conduct to ensure that it complies with all laws that affect its business.
