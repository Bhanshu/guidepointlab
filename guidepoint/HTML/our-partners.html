Community Service Partners

An integral part of our mission, GuidePoint Labs and its staff have affiliations and volunteer with a number of community organizations. We strive to make our local community a better place, to live, to work and to play.

Project HEAL

Project HEAL is an interdisciplinary medical service trip that provides acute care to rural and indigenous Ecuadorian villages, while simultaneously seeks to identify and address public health concerns through non-formal and participatory education in health, hygiene, and preventative practices. We aspire towards healthcare sustainability and work with an amazing group of UF and Ecuadorian physicians who are committed to international medicine, medical education, and cultural competence.

Dr. Shah, our GuidePoint Medical Director, goes to Ecuador every year with UF medical students to provide medical services to rural Ecuadorian communities. Learn more

Kids Kicking Cancer

Kids Kicking Cancer is a National Non-Profit with a very active local North Florida chapter. The nonprofit organization Kids Kicking Cancer helps children with cancer manage the stress and pain of their disease and treatments through personalized coachings instructed by black belt martial artists. These children achieve power, peace and purpose by teaching their deep breathing techniques to others to help them reduce stress.

Andrew Hix, GuidePoint Patient Coordinator, volunteers as a Martial Arts coach to implement the KKC program at Shands Hospital at the University of Florida in Gainesville. Learn more

Gainesville Area Innovation Network

GAIN mission is to support Entrepreneurs who want to change the world. The GAIN tagline is Inspire, Connect and Educate. GAIN serves to support all local North Florida entrepreneurs by providing a social network (GAINnet) and events designed to support entrepreneurs to become the backbone for economic development in our region.

LabCorp

LabCorp provides leading-edge medical laboratory tests and services through a national network of primary clinical laboratories and specialty testing laboratories.

With scientific expertise in esoteric testing, genomics, and clinical and anatomic pathology, LabCorp processes tests on approximately 470,000 specimens each day, applying advances in medicine and science to laboratory testing.

LabCorp operates a sophisticated laboratory network, with corporate headquarters in Burlington, NC, and more than 34,000 employees nationwide. Our 220,000 clients include physician offices, hospitals, managed care organizations, and biotechnology and pharmaceutical companies. Laboratory Corporation of America Holdings is listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) under ticker symbol LH.
