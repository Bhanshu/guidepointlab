Education Center

GuidePoint is committed to providing educational opportunities and resources for our clients, medical professionals and industry organizations to advance the practice of diagnostic and laboratory testing for the purpose of continually improving patient care

GuidePoint delivers education and resources by drawing on a network of Medical Professionals across specialties of Infectious Disease, Medical Oncology, Endocrinology, Global Health, Pediatrics and General Medicine.
